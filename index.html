How We Support Communities Across Kenya

As a volunteer-powered foundation operating in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru, we provide immediate community support with zero bureaucracy. From emergency shelter to school fees — every donation directly helps vulnerable Kenyans.

emergency shelter

Warm beds, hygiene kits, and outreach vans serving street families in Nairobi and Mombasa.

medical aid

Free consultations, medicines, and first aid through mobile clinics across Kenya.

orphan education

School fees, tutoring, and art programs for vulnerable children in Kisumu and Nakuru.

daily meals

Serving warm food in 6 locations across Kenya to combat hunger and malnutrition.

Frequently Asked Questions About Community Support in Kenya

How can I get help from StreetHelp in Kenya?

You can reach our community support team in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, or Nakuru through our contact page. We provide food assistance, medical aid, shelter support, and help for vulnerable people across Kenya.

Where does StreetHelp operate in Kenya?

StreetHelp provides community support in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and Garissa. We're expanding to reach more vulnerable communities across Kenya.

How can I donate to help vulnerable people in Kenya?

You can donate via PayPal using the buttons throughout our site. 100% of donations support food, medicine, shelter, and education programs for vulnerable Kenyans.
