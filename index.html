Common Questions

How can I get help?

Call our helpline at 0700 000 000 or visit any of our locations in Nairobi, Mombasa, or Kisumu. All services are free.

We currently serve communities in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and Garissa, with plans to expand.
